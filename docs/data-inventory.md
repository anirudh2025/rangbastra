# Data Inventory and Retention

| Data                                                                     | Location                                                        | Purpose                                                   | Access                                                                        | Recommended retention                                                                                               |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Auth identity, provider metadata                                         | Supabase Auth                                                   | Account access                                            | User; authorized owner via Supabase                                           | Active account plus 30 days after deletion request                                                                  |
| Profile name, email, phone, city, country, preferences, consent          | `profiles`                                                      | Private consultation continuity                           | Row owner; service role                                                       | Active account plus 30 days                                                                                         |
| Saved product IDs                                                        | `saved_inspirations`                                            | Wishlist continuity                                       | Row owner                                                                     | Until user removes or account deletion                                                                              |
| Customization contact details, dates, measurements, notes                | `customization_requests`                                        | Couture consultation                                      | Row owner; authorized owner                                                   | 12 months after closure unless contractual/legal need requires longer                                               |
| Reference images/PDFs and metadata                                       | private `customer-references` bucket and file table             | Design/measurement review                                 | Row owner; authorized owner                                                   | 90 days after request closure; shorter for measurement documents where feasible                                     |
| Verification notification status                                         | `customer_notifications`                                        | Reliable owner notification                               | Service role only                                                             | 90 days; remove `last_error` sooner if it can contain provider detail                                               |
| Product slug, user/anonymous pseudonymous ID, timestamp                  | `product_interest_events`                                       | Seven-day aggregate interest                              | Service role; aggregate RPC only                                              | 30 days via scheduled deletion                                                                                      |
| Product comments, private notes, display-name snapshot, moderation state | `product_comments`                                              | Moderated customer contribution and atelier communication | Approved public subset; row owner; authorized staff                           | Define operational retention; soft-delete promptly and purge on approved deletion request unless legal need applies |
| Official replies and moderation reports/audit                            | `comment_replies`, `moderation_reports`, `moderation_audit_log` | Owner response, safety review and accountability          | Relevant customer where applicable; authorized staff; approved replies public | Reports/audit per approved safety retention policy; replies follow parent comment                                   |
| Product ratings and likes                                                | `product_ratings`, `product_likes`                              | Honest aggregate rating and authenticated appreciation    | Rating aggregate public; owned records to customer; staff where required      | Until customer removes or account deletion                                                                          |
| Function/security logs                                                   | Supabase/Vercel                                                 | Operations and incident response                          | Least-privilege operators                                                     | 30–90 days based on provider controls                                                                               |

Do not put raw secrets, passwords, access/refresh tokens, full request bodies, file contents, measurements, or customer contact fields in logs or analytics. Consent timestamps are evidence and should be retained with the associated record. Account deletion must cover Auth, profile, saved items, owned requests where legally permissible, storage objects, and downstream provider records.
