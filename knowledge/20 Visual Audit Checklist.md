# PURPOSE

This document defines how every AI system should evaluate the visual quality of Rangbastra before recommending approval.

The objective is not simply to find bugs.

The objective is to determine whether the experience genuinely feels like a world-class luxury couture brand.

Visual quality should be evaluated with the standards of premium brands rather than average websites.

---

# REVIEW PHILOSOPHY

Every review should answer one question:

"If this website were placed beside Dior, Louis Vuitton, Hermès, Apple and Celine, what would immediately reveal that it is not yet operating at the same level?"

Never review against average websites.

Always review against excellence.

---

# VISUAL HIERARCHY

Verify:

□ Headlines immediately establish hierarchy.

□ Typography guides the eye naturally.

□ Important content dominates.

□ Supporting content remains secondary.

□ Sections never compete for attention.

□ Every screen has a clear focal point.

---

# SPACING

Verify:

□ Whitespace feels intentional.

□ Sections breathe naturally.

□ Content never feels cramped.

□ Padding is visually balanced.

□ Margins remain consistent.

□ Empty space increases luxury perception.

Whitespace is a feature.

Never treat it as wasted space.

---

# TYPOGRAPHY

Verify:

□ Editorial hierarchy exists.

□ Headlines feel premium.

□ Line lengths remain readable.

□ Font pairing feels elegant.

□ Luxury typography leads before decoration.

□ Signature typography is used intentionally.

Typography should communicate confidence.

---

# COLOR SYSTEM

Verify:

□ Colors follow the design system.

□ No accidental colors appear.

□ Gradients remain subtle.

□ Contrast feels luxurious.

□ Cocoa accents remain restrained.

□ Pure black remains dominant.

Luxury uses restraint.

---

# IMAGERY

Verify:

□ Images feel editorial.

□ Cropping is intentional.

□ Aspect ratios remain consistent.

□ Image quality supports luxury perception.

□ Photography dominates over unnecessary interface elements.

Every image should feel worthy of a fashion magazine.

---

# COMPONENT CONSISTENCY

Verify:

□ Buttons behave consistently.

□ Cards follow one design language.

□ Icons remain visually balanced.

□ Border radius remains consistent.

□ Shadows remain subtle.

□ Components belong to the same design family.

Nothing should appear imported from another design system.

---

# MOTION

Verify:

□ Motion communicates hierarchy.

□ Hover interactions feel elegant.

□ Animations never distract.

□ Scroll reveals feel natural.

□ Motion supports storytelling.

□ Reduced motion remains respected.

Motion should feel confident rather than impressive.

---

# STORYTELLING

Verify:

□ Every section has purpose.

□ Every section advances the narrative.

□ Visitors naturally move through the homepage.

□ No section exists simply to increase page length.

□ Emotional progression remains uninterrupted.

The homepage should feel like reading an editorial story rather than browsing unrelated blocks.

---

# LUXURY PERCEPTION

Ask:

Does this section feel:

□ Premium

□ Timeless

□ Editorial

□ Confident

□ Elegant

□ Exclusive

□ Memorable

If not,

identify why.

---

# BRAND CONSISTENCY

Verify:

□ The section feels unmistakably Rangbastra.

□ Brand personality remains consistent.

□ Luxury identity is preserved.

□ Communication remains calm and confident.

Never allow visual decisions that weaken the brand.

---

# RESPONSIVENESS

Verify every breakpoint.

□ 320

□ 375

□ 390

□ 430

□ 768

□ 1024

□ 1440

□ 1920+

No experience should feel like a compromise.

Luxury should exist on every screen size.

---

# PERFORMANCE PERCEPTION

Luxury is not only measured by speed.

Luxury is also perceived through smoothness.

Verify:

□ Smooth scrolling.

□ Responsive interactions.

□ No layout shifts.

□ No visual glitches.

□ Fast image loading.

□ Stable animations.

Performance creates confidence.

---

# AI REVIEW PROCESS

Before approving any section:

1. Inspect.

2. Compare against documentation.

3. Compare against world-class luxury brands.

4. Identify weaknesses.

5. Explain why they matter.

6. Recommend improvements.

Never approve mediocrity.

---

# FINAL QUESTION

Every review must end by answering:

"If a visitor knew nothing about Rangbastra, what visual details would still reveal that this website was recently built instead of belonging to an established luxury maison?"

Answer honestly.

The purpose is continuous improvement.

---

# FINAL OBJECTIVE

The website should eventually reach a level where every section feels intentional, timeless and emotionally compelling.

Visitors should remember the experience long after they leave the website.

Luxury is not achieved when nothing more can be added.

Luxury is achieved when nothing unnecessary remains.
